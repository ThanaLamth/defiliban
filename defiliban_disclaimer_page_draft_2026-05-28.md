Readers should not treat editorial coverage as a buy, sell, hold, stake, lend, trade, or legal-compliance recommendation.

Crypto and DeFi involve material risks, including but not limited to:

- smart contract failure
- exploits and security incidents
- liquidity risk
- custodial or counterparty risk

You should assume that losses can occur and perform your own due diligence before taking action.
