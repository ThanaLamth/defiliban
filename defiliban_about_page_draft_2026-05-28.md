## Page Copy

# About DeFiliban

DeFiliban is an independent digital publication focused on decentralized finance and the infrastructure that supports onchain markets.

We cover the systems, products, and risks shaping DeFi, with reporting and analysis built for readers who want more than headline-driven market noise. Our goal is to make complex developments easier to follow without stripping out the context that matters.

## What We Cover

DeFiliban follows the core areas of the DeFi ecosystem, including:

- protocols such as decentralized exchanges, lending markets, derivatives, and stablecoin systems
- yield across staking, farming, and strategy design
- liquidity trends, pools, AMMs, and capital flows
- risk, including exploits, liquidations, and smart contract failures
- infrastructure such as bridges, oracles, and Layer 2 networks

We also track major policy, market structure, and industry developments when they directly affect onchain users, builders, or capital allocation.

## Our Editorial Approach

We prioritize clarity, attribution, and context.

When reporting on a story, we aim to work from primary materials whenever possible, including protocol announcements, governance proposals, official statements, regulatory documents, exchange notices, public filings, and verifiable onchain or market data.

Our coverage is designed to help readers answer practical questions:

- what happened
- why it matters
- which protocol, product, or market participants are affected
- what readers should watch next

We aim to keep articles direct and readable, while preserving the technical and market details needed to understand a story properly.

## Accuracy and Updates

Crypto and DeFi move quickly, and facts can change after publication. When material details change, we update coverage to keep it accurate and useful for readers.

If we publish something inaccurate or incomplete, we review it and correct it as needed.
