# Contact

Contact DeFiliban for editorial questions, correction requests, verification issues, and general site inquiries related to our coverage of decentralized finance, digital assets, and market infrastructure.

## What this page is for

- Editorial questions about published coverage
- Requests for corrections or clarifications
- Team or author verification checks
- General business or partnership inquiries
- Technical issues affecting access to the website

## How to evaluate DeFiliban information

If your question is about how DeFiliban reports, reviews, and updates content, use these pages first:

- About: `/about/`
- Authors: `/authors/`
- Editorial Policy: `/editorial-policy/`
- Corrections Policy: `/corrections-policy/`
- Team Verification: `/team-verification/`
- Masthead: `/masthead/`

## Before reaching out

- For questions about a specific article, keep the article URL ready.
- For a correction request, note the exact sentence, figure, or claim you believe is inaccurate.
- For verification matters, use the author and masthead pages to confirm whether a profile or byline is official.

## Scope

DeFiliban reviews messages related to editorial accuracy, newsroom transparency, and site operations. Messages that do not include enough detail may take longer to review.
