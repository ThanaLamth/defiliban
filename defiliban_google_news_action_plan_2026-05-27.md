# DeFiliban Google News Action Plan

Date: 2026-05-27

## Must Fix Now

- Replace footer social placeholder links (`#`) with real social URLs or remove them.
- Strengthen the `About` page so the publisher identity is explicit and credible.
- Strengthen the `Contact` page with real publication contact details.
- Standardize author bio presence and quality across active news authors.
- Keep `rb-etemplate` excluded from sitemap and `noindex`.
- Prevent any return of attachment-style promo URLs or template junk.

## Should Fix This Week

- Add or improve an editorial/publisher information page.
- Review live category and archive pages used as news hubs and ensure they stay indexable, clean, and up to date.
- Confirm article pages consistently show:
  - title
  - author
  - publication date
  - modified date where relevant
- Audit remaining weak launch/listing/news URLs and decide:
  - keep
  - rewrite
  - noindex
  - remove
- Recrawl the site after purge and confirm the cleaned promo attachment URLs remain `404`.

## Content To Stop Publishing

- price prediction pages
- presale pages
- "best crypto to buy" style affiliate/promotional pages
- low-trust advertorial content
- sponsored-looking content without obvious disclosure
- duplicate attachment/media permalink junk

## Publishing Direction For The Next 2-4 Weeks

- publish clean, factual news content only
- keep article titles straightforward, not hype-driven
- avoid mixed commercial/news intent on main news sections
- maintain stable section pages such as `/news/` and `/crypto/`
- keep publishing cadence consistent rather than bursty

## Success Signals To Watch

- fewer low-value URLs entering discovery
- cleaner recrawl after purge
- GSC discovered-not-indexed count begins trending down after recrawl
- no new template or attachment junk URLs appear
- stronger consistency across author, about, and contact transparency
