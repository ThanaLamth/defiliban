# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DeFiliban may collect, use, and protect information when you visit `https://defiliban.io`.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

## Who We Are

DeFiliban is a digital publication focused on decentralized finance, crypto market structure, and blockchain infrastructure.

If you need to contact us about this policy or about personal data questions related to the site, please use our [Contact](https://defiliban.io/contact/) page.

## Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the site, DeFiliban may collect:

- information you submit directly, such as your name, email address, or message through contact forms
- information submitted through comments, if comments are enabled on the site
- technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, and basic usage data
- analytics or performance data used to understand traffic, site usage, and content engagement

## How We Use Information

Information collected through the site may be used to:

- operate, secure, and maintain the website
- respond to contact requests or reader questions
- review comments, moderation activity, or abuse prevention signals
- improve site performance, usability, and editorial publishing workflows
- understand traffic and content usage patterns

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

DeFiliban may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality, analytics, performance, login/session behavior, comment handling, or preference storage.

If comments, login areas, embedded media, analytics tools, or advertising tools are active, those features may place cookies in your browser or process related technical data.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings.

## Comments

If comments are enabled and you leave a comment on the site, we may collect the data shown in the comment form, along with technical data such as IP address and browser information for moderation, spam prevention, and site security purposes.

If a third-party profile or avatar service is connected to comments, related data may also be processed through that service according to its own policy.

## Contact Forms and Direct Messages

If you contact DeFiliban through a form or other direct site-based contact method, we may retain the information you submit so we can review and respond to your request.

Please do not send sensitive personal, financial, or account-security information through ordinary contact forms unless it is necessary.

## Embedded Content and Third-Party Services

Articles or pages on DeFiliban may include embedded content such as videos, charts, social posts, images, or other media hosted by third parties.

When you interact with embedded content, those third-party services may collect data, set cookies, or track interactions according to their own policies.

DeFiliban may also use third-party infrastructure, analytics, security, performance, form, or spam-prevention services where needed to operate the site.

## Analytics and Site Performance

DeFiliban may use analytics or measurement tools to understand site traffic, audience behavior, and content performance.

These tools may collect device, browser, traffic-source, and page-interaction information in aggregated or service-processed form.

## Data Retention

We may retain submitted messages, moderation records, technical logs, analytics records, or related site data for as long as reasonably necessary to operate, secure, improve, or document the site and its publishing systems.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

## Data Sharing

DeFiliban does not describe this policy as a promise never to share data under any circumstance. Data may be processed or disclosed where reasonably necessary to:

- run the site through service providers or infrastructure partners
- prevent abuse, fraud, spam, or security issues
- comply with applicable legal obligations or lawful requests
- protect the rights, safety, or operations of the publication

## Your Choices

Depending on your location and the nature of your interaction with the site, you may have the ability to:

- avoid submitting optional information through forms or comments
- disable or limit cookies through your browser settings
- request clarification about information you have submitted through the site

If you have a privacy-related request tied to information you directly submitted, use the [Contact](https://defiliban.io/contact/) page.

## Children's Privacy

DeFiliban is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children in a way that violates applicable law.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the site inappropriately, contact us so the issue can be reviewed.

## Changes to This Policy

DeFiliban may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the site, its tools, or its operational practices change.

The most current version should be treated as the controlling version for the website.
